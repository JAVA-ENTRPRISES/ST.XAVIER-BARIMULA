Our Heritage

Established in 2026 in the vibrant city of Kendrapara, ST. XAVIERS SCHOOL was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional values and modern technological advancement. Located in Patia, our campus provides a serene environment conducive to intellectual growth.

We believe that every child is unique. Our approach is centered around "Project Based Learning" (PBL), ensuring that students don't just memorize facts but understand their application in the real world.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution that nurtures globally competent citizens who are rooted in Indian values and ethics.

Our Mission

To provide an environment that encourages curiosity, rewards creativity, and promotes a culture of lifelong learning through a balanced CBSE curriculum.
