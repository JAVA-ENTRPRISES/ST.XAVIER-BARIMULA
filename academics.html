Our Curriculum Structure

Following the latest CBSE guidelines for 2026, our academic program is divided into four key stages to ensure age-appropriate cognitive development.

Foundational Stage (Nursery to Class II)

Focus on play-based and activity-based learning. Developing basic literacy, numeracy, and social skills.

English Oral Number Work Art & Craft EVS

Preparatory Stage (Class III to V)

Introduction to formal classroom learning with a focus on core subjects and discovery-based learning.

Mathematics Science Social Studies Computer Coding

Middle Stage (Class VI to VIII)

Subject-oriented pedagogical learning. Abstract concepts in sciences and humanities are introduced.

Physics Chemistry Sanskrit/Odia Robotics

Secondary Stage (Class IX to X)

Preparation for Board Examinations with deep dive into disciplinary areas and critical thinking.

Advanced Math Social Sciences Information Technology Languages
